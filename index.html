EXPECTATIONS

It was a week-long trip to Kenya with the compagny African Eagle. I was both excited to go and skeptical. Indeed, going to a continent like Africa was a first for me. Everyone talks about Africa, but few people have actually been there. Going on safari was a dream for me, seeing landscapes and animals in their natural, wild state is rare these days. It was an opportunity that couldn't be missed!

SAFARI

In Kenya, five animals are considered the most important: lion, rhinoceros, buffalo, elephant and leopard. We call them the big five. Visiting Kenya's nature reserves made me realize just how beautiful our world really is. The landscapes were endless. The mix of colors was splendid. I was also able to swim in the Indian Ocean. The water was transparent and the marine animals were all unique. Kenya reminded me of the films Nemo and The Lion King. It took me back to my childhood. I have fond memories of this trip and I urge you to give it a try.
